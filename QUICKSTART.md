Get your NFT Avatar Zone up and running in 2 minutes!

## Step 1: Open the App

Simply open `index.html` in your browser. That's it! No installation needed.

## Step 2: Upload Your Photo

1. Click or drag & drop your photo onto the upload area
2. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP (max 10MB)

## Step 3: Choose a Character

1. Browse the character templates on the right sidebar
2. Filter by category if you want (Zombie, Demon, Ghost, etc.)
3. Click any character to merge it with your photo

## Step 4: Customize

Use the sliders to adjust:
- **Zoom**: Make your face bigger or smaller
- **Rotation**: Rotate your face
- **Position X/Y**: Move your face around
- **Brightness/Contrast/Saturation**: Adjust colors

## Step 5: Export

- **Download**: Save as PNG to your device
- **Share**: Share on social media
- **Mint NFT**: Try the demo minting experience
